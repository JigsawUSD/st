# **App Name**: Baby Bites Hub

## Core Features:

- Recipe Catalog: Browse a curated selection of baby food recipes suitable for different age groups and dietary needs.
- Age Filtering: Filter recipes based on the baby's age to ensure appropriateness and safety.
- Ingredient Search: Search for recipes based on available ingredients.
- AI Recipe Generator: Generate new baby food recipes based on available ingredients. The AI tool checks for age appropriateness.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft pastel green (#A7D9A7) to convey a sense of health and natural goodness.
- Background color: Very light beige (#F5F5DC) to provide a warm and inviting feel.
- Accent color: Light orange (#FFB347) to highlight important elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif for readability.
- Use cute and simple icons related to food, babies, and cooking.
- Clean and organized layout with plenty of whitespace for easy navigation.
- Gentle animations and transitions to create a playful and engaging experience.
